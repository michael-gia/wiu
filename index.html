Servicman United

Project Outreach

Serviceman United's project outreach is a program designed to help unite the men and women who have taken time to serve their country. This program is here to help rehabilitate and transition combat veterans from the United States Armed Forces to an active, healthy, and successful civilian life. We also offer programs and services to help struggling and homeless veterans to get back on their feet.
